Taichan Skewers

Description

A very popular type of chicken skewers in Indonesia, want to make it at home? Follow the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 200 gr ayam paha atas tanpa tulang
  • 1 tsp bubuk bawang putih
  • 1 tsp garam
  • 1 buah jeruk nipis

Sambal Ingridients

  • 20 red chilies
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 30 ml water

Steps

  • Blend chili, water and salt
  • Mix chicken with garlic powder and lime Let the chicken sit for about 5 minutes until the spices are absorbed Grilled chicken
  • After it's cooked,mix it with the chili
